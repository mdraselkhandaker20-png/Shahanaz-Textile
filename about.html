Our story

Built on a dream.
Named for a mother.

Shahanaz Textile is more than a business — it is a promise kept to a family, and a dream that started long before the first stitch.

Our parents worked hard all their lives. Through their jobs, they built a home, raised a family, and put their children through school and university. We grew up watching them — their dedication, their sacrifices, their quiet determination.

When a teacher once asked what our dream was, the answer came without hesitation: "Garments. A factory. And salaries paid on the 1st of every month."

Because we know what it means when a salary is late. We have seen it. We will never let that happen to the people who work with us.

In 2026, we took the first step. We named this business after our mother — Shahanaz — because everything we are, we owe to her.

The factory is still a dream in progress. But the office is real. The work is real. And the vision is very much alive.

The dream ahead

We are building toward something bigger — a full garments industry, rooted in Narshingdi, Bangladesh.

A factory next to home — where quality is made, not just promised

Salaries paid on the 1st of every month, without exception

Jobs for people in our community

A brand that carries our mother's name with pride
