Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

Ordering

What is the minimum order quantity?

+

The minimum order is 10 pieces per order. This applies to both catalog products and custom orders. You can mix sizes within one order — for example, 3×S, 4×M, 3×L counts as 10 pcs.

Can I order different item types together?

+

Yes! Our order form lets you add multiple item types in a single order — for example, Round Neck + Polo + Muslimah Long Sleeve all in one submission. Each item type has its own size and quantity row.

How do I place an order?

+

Two ways:

  • Catalog order — Browse our products, click Order, fill in the form and send via WhatsApp.
  • Custom order — Chat with us on WhatsApp with your design or idea. We create a mockup for your approval, then you fill in the order form.
In both cases, you will receive a full job sheet PDF before any payment is required.
Payment

How does payment work?

+

We use a two-part payment system:

  • 50% upfront — paid when you confirm your order
  • 50% on delivery — paid when you receive your order
No payment is required until we confirm your order details and you are satisfied with the job sheet.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept:

  • bKash
  • Nagad
  • Bank transfer
  • Cash on delivery (available for select areas)
Delivery

How long does delivery take?

+

Delivery takes 3 to 7 working days after production is complete. Delivery time may vary depending on your location within Bangladesh.

Production begins only after order confirmation and 50% upfront payment.

Do you deliver nationwide?

+

Yes, we deliver to all districts across Bangladesh via courier service.

Design & Custom orders

I don't have a design — can you create one for me?

+

Absolutely. Share your idea, theme, or any reference image with us on WhatsApp — we will create a mockup for your approval. Production only begins once you are happy with the design.

What file formats do you accept for designs?

+

We accept JPG, PNG, and PDF files. For best print quality, high-resolution files (300 DPI or above) are recommended. You can also share a Google Drive or WeTransfer link.

Can I order different colours for the same design?

+

Yes. In our order form, each item row has its own colour and neck colour selection. You can add multiple rows for the same design in different colours.

Returns & quality

What is your return and replacement policy?

+

We stand behind the quality of everything we produce. If there is an issue with your order:

  • Wrong size, torn, or damaged items — we will replace those specific pieces at no charge
  • Repairable items — we will repair them if possible
  • Issues must be reported within 48 hours of receiving your order
Please check your order upon delivery and contact us immediately via WhatsApp if anything is wrong.

What if the print quality is not as expected?

+

We approve a job sheet with mockup before production. If the final product significantly differs from the approved mockup, we will work with you to resolve it — including reprinting if necessary.
